How I started do that ..

It all started with a banal Windows Paint when I needed to minimally edit the photo in my years of study .

Subsequently, having bought a simple camera, I realized that taking and editing photos is not only my hobby but something more. Paint of course, was not able to meet my requirements, and I found a lot of interesting programs such as Canva, AdobePhotoshop, Vsco and others. Having tried myself in them, I felt that I could help people with photo processing or restoration of old and damaged photos.

I also have the skills to create business cards, postcards, calendars and various trifles. Call for help, I'm glad to help You!
